Taxation and Credit Redistribution System

Overview

The H.O.R.I.Z.O.N operates under a unified credit-based economy. To ensure fair distribution of resources, development of the nation, and sustainability of shared infrastructure, all citizens and operations are subject to a structured tax system. All rates are percentages taken from earned or stored credits.

Income Tax

Applied automatically to all contributions and jobs completed. This funds defense, expansion, and public services.

  • Standard Income Tax: 10%
  • High Yield Income (1000+ credits): 15%
  • Low-Income Bracket (under 100 credits): 5%

Business & Trade Tax

Citizens operating public shops, trade networks, or service facilities must contribute a small portion of profit.

  • Public Stall Operations: 8%
  • Private Trade Hubs: 12%
  • Guild-based Commerce: Flat rate of 50 credits/week

Resource Tax

For large-scale extraction operations (mining, harvesting, refining), a one-time tax based on rarity and volume is applied. Individuals collecting casually are not taxed under this category.

  • Bulk Iron/Stone: 5% of collected value
  • Diamond/Ancient Debris: 15% of collected value
  • High-Yield Automation Farms: Custom tax reviewed per build

Usage & Enforcement

All taxes are collected automatically during credit transactions. Avoiding tax through unauthorized trade or illegal shops is punishable by credit removal or Black Guard enforcement. Transparency in taxation builds loyalty and strength.
